Ecostage

Ecostage is the go-to site for practical guidelines and ecological values for the performing industry.

Formerly known as Ecostage Pledge, launched in 2015, Ecostage is an autonomous online platform to help creative practitioners implement a more ecologically- conscious approach in performance at the making stage. We have a crowdfunding page as we are creating a new website full of useful resources to create a network for positive sustainable change in the performing industry worldwide.


The website will contain:

  • A list of principles to be used as sustainable guidelines in the performance production process.
  • An Ecostage pledge with a logo to download and add to documents; making your sustainable intent visible.
  • An expansive library of resources, including case studies that celebrate diverse practices that show 'ecoscenography in action'.
  • An approach to performance production that acknowledges the whole ecosystem.
  • An online supportive community of likeminded practitioners.

We invite you to take the pledge and manifest your commitment to a sustainable future.
