Tartan Heart Festival, Belladrum

Black Dog Puppet Company, lead by Greg Dobson, have brought many exciting life-size puppets to the Tartan Heart Festival over the past few years. I have orked as part of the team to operate multiple War Horse replica puppets, a life-sized elephant puppet and a life-sized whale puppet. We have also performed at several of the Ness Islands Halloween Shows.
